About Us

Titan Watches

When Titan Company Limited launched its quartz watches some 30 years ago, the founders – a merry bunch of Tata employees who started out simply wanting ‘to do something different’ – could not have foreseen just how completely they would capture the imagination of Indian consumers in the post-liberalization era of the 1990s.

Sonata Watches

Sonata watches, from the house of Tata, are known for being India's largest-selling watch brand, offering a wide range of trendy, fashionable, and affordable timepieces for every occasion. The brand focuses on providing self-expression through diverse styles, from minimalist to bold designs, while maintaining commitment to quality and affordability.
